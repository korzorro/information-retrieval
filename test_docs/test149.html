Felinology

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Felinology is the study of cats.[1] The term has a Latin-Greek origin and comes from the Latin word felinus (of cats, feline) and the Greek -logos (science). Felinology is concerned with studying the anatomy, genetics, physiology, and breeding of domestic and wild cats.
