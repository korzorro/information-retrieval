Category:Invasive mammal species

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
For more information, see Invasive species.

This category is for articles and lists dealing specifically with the introduction, by man, of mammals to an area where they are not native. This category should not contain general articles about species, only articles concerned specifically with the species' introductions. For additional information, see Wikipedia:Categories for discussion/Log/2007 May 23#Category:Introduced Species.
